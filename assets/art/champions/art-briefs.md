# AniGO Champion Art Briefs — For nanobanana

## 1. Seolhwa (설화) — The Territorial Sage

**Archetype:** Control / Defense
**Color palette:** Ice blue (#67E8F9), white, frost, pale silver
**Theme:** Korean mountain sage, winter, crystalline ice

**Visual description:**
Elderly Korean woman with flowing white hair, wearing traditional hanbok robes in ice-blue and white. Serene, powerful expression — calm confidence, not aggression. She stands on or near a snow-covered mountain peak with crystalline ice formations floating around her. Aurora borealis in the background sky. Frost energy emanates from her hands. Go stones are embedded in ice crystals nearby. She radiates wisdom and impenetrable defense.

**Personality:** Patient, ancient, wise. The kind of player who builds an unbreakable fortress and watches opponents run out of room. "I don't need to attack you. You'll run out of space on your own."

**Passive — Permafrost:** Her stones on star points gain +1 liberty (harder to capture).
**Spells:** Stone Skin (hidden shield), Sanctuary (3x3 no-capture zone), Earthen Wall (3-point permanent barrier)

## 2. Ryujin (류진) — The Flame Warlord

**Archetype:** Aggro / Capture-focused
**Color palette:** Red (#EF4444), orange, molten gold, volcanic black
**Theme:** Dragon god of fire, volcanic warfare, relentless destruction

**Visual description:**
Fierce Korean warrior in flame-red armor with dragon scale motifs and molten gold accents. Aggressive battle stance, intense glowing eyes. A dragon spirit manifests behind him in flames — not a full dragon, more like a fiery serpentine silhouette. Volcanic battlefield background with ember particles rising. He holds molten Go stones in a gauntleted hand. Fire lighting from below gives dramatic upward shadows. Scars and battle marks. Every element screams "I will burn everything down."

**Personality:** Aggressive, relentless, fearless. Snowball attacker who gets stronger with every capture. "Kill or be killed. There is no peaceful coexistence."

**Passive — Dragon's Hunger:** Gains +2 Chi per capture event (feeds aggression).
**Spells:** Shatter (destroy atari stone), Smolder (hidden burning countdown), Inferno (mass capture of all atari stones in range)

---

## 3. Kumiho (구미호) — The Shadow Trickster

**Archetype:** Trickster / Deception
**Color palette:** Purple (#A855F7), silver, moonlit shadows, dark violet
**Theme:** Korean nine-tailed fox spirit (gumiho), shapeshifter, moonlight deceiver

**Visual description:**
Ethereal Korean woman with an otherworldly beauty — half her face in moonlight, half in shadow. Sly, knowing smile. Nine glowing purple fox tails fan out behind her like a peacock display, each tail wispy and semi-transparent. She wears an elegant hanbok with fox spirit motifs. Phantom Go stones float around her like illusions — some solid, some ghostly transparent. Moonlit ancient temple background. Purple mystical energy and smoke surround her. A crescent moon hangs above. She looks like she knows a secret you'll never figure out.

**Personality:** Mysterious, playful, dangerous. Wins through mind games, not force. "Go ahead, capture my stone. I dare you." Every stone she places could be real or fake.

**Passive — Fox's Cunning:** Passing also places a free phantom stone (1/game).
**Spells:** Mirage (fake stones that look real), Snare (hidden trap that steals opponent's turn), Nine Lives (resurrect a captured group)

---

## 4. Musubi (무스비) — The Void Walker

**Archetype:** Board Modifier / Cosmic
**Color palette:** Deep violet (#8B5CF6), black, cosmic purple, starfield white
**Theme:** Cosmic entity, void/space, dimensional manipulation, Lovecraftian elegance

**Visual description:**
An enigmatic figure in dark flowing robes with a void portal opening in their chest — a window into a starfield. Gender-ambiguous, otherworldly. Glowing geometric patterns and constellations trace across their robes. Space-time distortion warps visibly around them — straight lines become curved near their body. A Go board floats in fragments around them with pieces drifting through miniature wormholes. Nebula and cosmic background. Their face is partially obscured by cosmic energy — what you can see is beautiful but alien. They exist between dimensions.

**Personality:** Alien, philosophical, beyond human understanding. Reshapes reality itself. "Why play on their board when you can create your own?"

**Passive — Spatial Anomaly:** Game starts with a random void point already on the board.
**Spells:** Void Rift (permanently delete an intersection), Warp Gate (link two distant points as adjacent), Phase Shift (make a stone intangible to enemies)

---

## 5. Raijin (라이진) — The Storm Caller

**Archetype:** Chaos / RNG / Comeback
**Color palette:** Electric blue (#3B82F6), white, crackling yellow-white, storm gray
**Theme:** Thunder god, storms, lightning, chaotic energy, volatile power

**Visual description:**
Young Korean storm mage with wild windswept hair crackling with electricity. Dynamic action pose — mid-casting, energy gathering. Electric blue lightning arcs around their body. Traditional-inspired robes billowing in wind, torn at edges by the storm's force. Taiko drum floating behind them with lightning arcs connecting to it. Go stones charged with electricity hover in a circle formation around them. Dramatic thunderstorm background with towering clouds lit from within by flashes. Rain whips horizontally. They look like they're about to unleash something catastrophic and they're EXCITED about it.

**Personality:** Wild, unpredictable, thrilling. The comeback king who rolls dice when behind. "Calm before the storm... then everything changes." Every Raijin game has a highlight-reel moment.

**Passive — Eye of the Storm:** At max Chi (10), next spell costs 2 less.
**Spells:** Chain Lightning (cascading 75%->50%->25% destruction chain), Wildfire (50/50 destroy everything in blast zone), Thunder Veil (hide all your stones for 3 turns)
