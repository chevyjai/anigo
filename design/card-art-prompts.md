# AniGO Card Art — AI Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts with an AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Gemini) to create Yu-Gi-Oh!/Hearthstone quality card art.

**Style guide for ALL images:**
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait, 768x1024px minimum)
- Style: anime/manga-inspired digital painting, similar to Yu-Gi-Oh! card art
- Lighting: dramatic, cinematic, high contrast
- Background: atmospheric, not plain — fits the character's element
- Quality: detailed, professional illustration, TCG card art quality

## Card Frame Design

After generating art, each card needs a frame:
- **Champion cards**: Gold metallic ornate border (like Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultra Rare), holographic shimmer
- **Spell cards**: Category-colored border (red for offensive, teal for defensive, purple for info, green for terrain, amber for trap)
- **Chi cost**: Diamond badge in top-right with the number
- **Name plate**: Dark banner at bottom with gold text
- **Rarity stars**: Bottom-center (all champions are 5-star rarity)

1. Generate images using the prompts above
