# AniGO Visual Direction — 2.5D Reference

**Reference image**: `/Users/chevan_tin/anigo/design/visual-reference.png`

## Key Visual Elements from Reference

### Board
- **2.5D isometric perspective** — board viewed at ~30-40° angle, not flat top-down
- **Dark slate/stone material** — NOT wood. Dark gray-blue stone with subtle texture
- **Mystical rune engravings** — circular arcane pattern in the center of the board, faint runic borders
- **Cyan/teal grid lines** — not warm gray, but cool glowing cyan lines on dark stone
- **Board has physical depth** — thick slab with visible edge/side, casting shadow on table surface
- **Table surface visible** — dark wood table beneath the board, slight depth of field blur

### Stones
- **Physical, glossy, 3D-looking** — strong specular highlight, visible reflection
- **Black stones**: Dark with GREEN/TEAL shimmer (not blue) — emerald iridescent quality
- **White stones**: Pearlescent cream/white with soft glow — almost translucent
- **Both**: Cast shadows on board, slight elevation above surface (2.5D depth)
- **Stones look like real glass/jade pieces** — premium, collectible feel

### Void Rift Effect
- **Purple/violet starburst** in the center — dramatic, dark, crystalline crack pattern
- **Looks like the board itself is shattered** — not just an overlay, the board material is broken
- **Purple glow emanating** from the void — ambient purple light on surrounding area

### Spell Cards (Right Panel)
- **Card-game style** — each spell is a proper illustrated card with art
- **Cyan/teal glow borders** — cards have glowing energy borders
- **Small Chi cost badge** in top corner (diamond shape, number inside)
- **Card names below** the art (Shatter, Wildfire, Snare)
- **Each card has a unique icon** — fire for Wildfire, crystal/cross for Snare, etc.
- **Cards are slightly fanned** — overlapping, like holding a hand of cards

### Chi Counter
- **Diamond/crystal shape** — golden amber crystal icon
- **Large prominent number** (7) inside the crystal
- **Warm gold/amber color** — stands out against the cool blue-gray palette

### Score Panels (Black/White)
- **Glassmorphism cards** — semi-transparent with subtle blur
- **Stone preview** in each panel — a mini stone showing whose score it is
- **Clean layout**: captures + territory icons with numbers
- **Particle/sparkle effects** around the panels

### Color Palette (Updated from Reference)
- **Board**: Dark slate #1a1e2e (cool dark, not warm walnut)
- **Grid lines**: Glowing cyan #00d4d4 at low opacity
- **Black stones**: #0d0d12 with green shimmer #2dd4a8
- **White stones**: #f0ead6 with pearlescent glow
- **Spell accent**: Cyan/teal #00c8ff (not warm gold for spells — gold only for Chi)
- **Void**: Deep purple #6b21a8 with violet glow
- **Chi**: Amber gold #D4A017 (kept from original)
- **Background**: Deep dark #08080f with dark wood table texture

### Overall Mood
- **Mystical, premium, physical** — like an enchanted Go set in a dark library
- **Cool color temperature** — predominantly cyan/teal/purple with gold Chi accent
- **The board feels REAL** — like you could reach out and pick up the stones
- **Magic is subtle but present** — glowing grid, shimmer on stones, void effects
